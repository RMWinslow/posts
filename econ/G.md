A few things to note:
- Housing and Community Services is mostly water, electricity, waste management.
- The market value of Gov purchases for GDP accounting is often just imputed as the cost of the input. 
    - For example, the salaries of IRS employees are included under "tax collection" and the salaries of many social workers are included under "Income Security".
