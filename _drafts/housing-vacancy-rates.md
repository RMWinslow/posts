# Housing Vacancy Rates

Counter to the weird reddit narrative, vacant homes are not a sign of injustice in the presence of homeless, but rather something that needs to exist to prevent homelessness in the first place.

Just like job seekers are better off when there are job vacancies, home-seekers are better off when there are home vacancies.

Vacancies provide the "slack" required for a functioning market where people can actually move.
Without vacancies, any change in life circumstances (new job, growing family) becomes impossible to accommodate.
